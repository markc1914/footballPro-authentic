## Play Calling Tips

### On Offense:
- **1st & 10**: Try a run to set up play action
- **2nd & Long**: Short pass to get yards back
- **3rd & Short**: QB sneak or inside run
- **3rd & Long**: Medium or deep pass
- **Red Zone**: Power run or play action

### On Defense:
- **1st Down**: Base 4-3 (stop the run)
- **2nd & Long**: Nickel with blitz
- **3rd & Short**: Goal line defense
- **3rd & Long**: Cover 3 zone, rush 4
- **Red Zone**: Man coverage, bring pressure
